## Implementation Guidelines

### For Best Results:

1. **Unsplash Quality:**
   - Use high-resolution images (suggested URL format already includes `w=2000`)
   - Prefer images with clear focal points
   - Avoid overly busy compositions
   - Consider mobile cropping - important elements should be center-weighted

2. **Consistency:**
   - Maintain similar tonal range within AI & Automation category
   - Mix concrete and abstract imagery for variety
   - Avoid repeating the same Unsplash photographer's work too frequently

3. **Testing:**
   - Preview images at different screen sizes
   - Check social media card previews (Twitter/LinkedIn)
   - Ensure text overlays (if any) remain readable

4. **Metadata:**
   - Update `ogImage` field when changing `featureImage`
   - Consider updating `metaDescription` if image changes the framing

### Batch Processing Approach:

**Priority 1 (Do First):**
- Fix the 3 clearly mismatched images (#1-3)
- Address high-profile featured posts currently missing images

**Priority 2 (Quick Wins):**
- Posts with "No image" that are featured or recent
- Posts where a small tweak to current image would significantly improve

**Priority 3 (Long Tail):**
- Older posts with marginal improvements
- Posts where current image is "okay but could be better"
