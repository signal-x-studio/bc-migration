Year-One Operating Plan

Strategic roadmap for the first Product Architect role at BigCommerce

Role: Product Architect (First in Role) Reports To: CPO Start: January 2026

The Job

You were hired to solve a specific problem: BigCommerce has grown through acquisition (Core + Makeswift + Feedonomics) but lacks architectural coherence. The products don't talk to each other well, and this blocks both the Enterprise value proposition and the AI-native future.

Your Four Responsibilities

1. Diagnose what's actually broken (not what people think is broken)
2. Architect the path forward (make the hard technical calls)
3. Influence execution across teams you don't control
4. Deliver visible wins that justify the role

Success Criteria

What "Good" Looks Like by Year End

Established Credibility

bc-migration shipped, announced at Summit, in production use

Mapped Integration Debt

Completed diagnostic across all 7 dimensions with real data

Made First Major Call

Semantic Layer approach decided and funded

Built Relationships

Engineering leads, Product leads see you as useful

What You Will NOT Have Done

Rewritten Anything Major

Too early - foundation first

Built a Team

You're a single-threaded owner with cohorts

Solved Integration

That's a multi-year effort

Shipped AI Features

Beyond migration - foundation first
