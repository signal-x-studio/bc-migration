BC-Migration: April Summit Workback Plan

12-week execution plan to deliver bc-migration announcement at the April summit

Target: April 2026 Summit Owner: Nino Chavez

The Offer: What We're Announcing

BigCommerce's Offer to WooCommerce Merchants

"Migrate in 48 hours. Keep your SEO rankings. Gain access to every marketplace. Pay nothing until you're live."

Per Rick Watson's NRF 2026 analysis: "It's not the protocol or technology that is the thing. It all comes down to the offer." Our Summit messaging must lead with merchant outcomes, not technical capabilities.

What Merchants Get Why It Matters How We Deliver
Zero-downtime migration No lost sales during transition bc-migration + WPEngine partnership
SEO preservation Protect existing organic traffic URL mapping, redirect automation
Marketplace access Sell on Amazon, Google, Facebook instantly Feedonomics integration included
Trusted partner support Not a cold migration tool WPEngine co-branded experience
