Strategic Context

BigCommerce is at an inflection point:

Leadership Changes

Travis Hess (CEO) and Daniel Lentz (CFO/COO) recruited you specifically for this initiative. The CFO/COO consolidation signals a focus on operational efficiency and margin improvement.

Signal Implication for Product Architecture
CFO/COO dual role (Lentz) Every architectural decision must pass a financial ROI test
WPEngine partnership bc-migration is customer acquisition strategy, not just tooling
CPO's AI-native vision Architecture must enable AI capabilities, not just features
Siloed acquisitions Integration debt is blocking Enterprise value proposition

Your Operating Model

You're a single-threaded owner with cohorts across the organization. Success requires:

Influence Without Authority

  • Engineering cohorts execute your vision
  • Partnership team drives WPEngine alignment
  • Marketing owns Summit content
  • Sales recruits beta merchants

Translate Constantly

  • To Finance: margin improvement, cost reduction
  • To Engineering: technical architecture, patterns
  • To Sales: deal enablement, competitive positioning
  • To Executives: strategic impact, risk mitigation

Integration Diagnostic: 7 Dimensions

The diagnostic framework assesses integration debt across these dimensions:

Dimension Focus Area
1. Identity & Access Single sign-on, permissions across products
2. Data & Schema Data models, synchronization, canonical entities
3. API & Contracts API consistency, versioning, developer experience
4. Infrastructure Deployment, observability, shared services
5. User Experience Navigation, design systems, workflows
6. Billing & Licensing Packaging, metering, unified billing
7. Organizational Team structure, ownership, roadmap coordination
