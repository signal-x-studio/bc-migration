BC Migration Documentation

Complete guide to migrating your WooCommerce store to BigCommerce while preserving your WordPress site, theme, and content.

What is BC Migration?

BC Migration is a comprehensive toolkit that enables you to migrate from WooCommerce to BigCommerce as your commerce backend while keeping your WordPress site intact. This headless commerce approach allows you to:

  • Maintain your existing WordPress site, theme, and content
  • Preserve SEO rankings and URL structures
  • Migrate products, customers, categories, and orders automatically
  • Reduce migration time from weeks to days
  • Scale beyond WooCommerce limitations without rebuilding your site

Key Concepts

Headless Commerce

BC Migration enables a headless commerce architecture where BigCommerce serves as the commerce backend (products, cart, checkout) while WordPress continues to serve as the frontend (content, pages, theme). This separation provides scalability and flexibility.

Zero-Downtime Migration

The migration process is designed to minimize downtime. You can migrate data incrementally and test thoroughly before switching traffic to BigCommerce.

Data Preservation

All critical data is preserved during migration: products, variants, categories, customers, and historical orders. SEO metadata, URLs, and content remain in WordPress.
