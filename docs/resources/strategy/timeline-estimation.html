Timeline Estimation

Guide to estimating realistic timelines for WooCommerce to BigCommerce migration.

Timeline Factors

  • Data volume (products, customers, orders)
  • Store complexity
  • Custom functionality
  • Team size and experience
  • Testing requirements

Typical Timelines

Small Store (<500 products)

  • Assessment: 1 day
  • Migration: 1-2 days
  • Validation: 1 day
  • Total: 3-4 days

Medium Store (500-5000 products)

  • Assessment: 2 days
  • Migration: 3-5 days
  • Validation: 2 days
  • Total: 7-9 days

Large Store (>5000 products)

  • Assessment: 3-5 days
  • Migration: 5-10 days
  • Validation: 3-5 days
  • Total: 11-20 days
