Persona Analysis

Migration Personas

Merchant archetypes for targeted migration strategies and platform recommendations

Overview

Not all WooCommerce merchants are the same. This persona analysis segments merchants by business model, technical sophistication, and operational needs to provide tailored migration recommendations. Understanding which persona(s) apply helps scope the migration correctly and set appropriate expectations.

Persona-Based Migration

Merchants often exhibit characteristics of multiple personas. The dominant persona drives the primary migration approach, while secondary traits inform customization needs and risk factors.

Source: Granite Analysis, Slides 121-172

Persona Identification Checklist

Use this checklist during discovery to identify the merchant's primary and secondary personas:

Business Model Questions

  • What percentage of revenue comes from B2B vs B2C customers?
  • Do you sell on multiple channels (Amazon, eBay, social)?
  • Do you have subscription or recurring revenue products?
  • Do you operate in multiple countries or currencies?
  • Do you have franchisees or multiple locations?

Technical Complexity Questions

  • How many products and variants do you manage?
  • Do you have ERP, CRM, or other critical integrations?
  • Do you use price lists or customer-specific pricing?
  • How important is WordPress to your content strategy?
  • What is your peak traffic and order volume?

Pain Point Validation

  • What are your top 3 frustrations with WooCommerce?
  • What features do you wish you had today?
  • How much time does your team spend on maintenance?
  • Have you experienced security or performance incidents?
  • What triggered your interest in platform migration?
